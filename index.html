PUNJAB

Punjab is a geopolitical, cultural, and historical region in South Asia, specifically in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent. The geographical definition of the term "Punjab" has changed over time. In the 16th century Mughal Empire it referred to a relatively smaller area between the Indus and the Sutlej rivers. The GDP of Punjab region mainly depends upon agriculture.

The main religions in Punjab region are Islam(60%), Hindu(28%) and Sikh(12%). Language spoken in this region is Punjabi, which is written using both Gurmukhi and Shahmukhi script. Lahore has been the capital of Punjab region since the establishment of Sikh empire.
